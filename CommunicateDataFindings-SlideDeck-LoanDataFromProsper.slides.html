Investigation Overview¶

I am going to communicate the findings of the prosper Loan dataset by showing some visualizations with description. I will provide some details about the loans length, Lender yield, Effective yield, Estimated loss, and estimated return and their relation with category of the listing and the loan status

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset is available in https://s3.amazonaws.com/udacity-hosted-downloads/ud651/prosperLoanData.csv.

This data set contains 113,937 loans with 81 variables on each loan, including loan amount, borrower rate (or interest ate), current loan status, borrower income, and many others.

Univariate Exploration: Term Feature¶

Definition: The length of the loan expressed in months.

In [10]:
sb.countplot(data = df_after200907, x = 'Term', color = default_color).set_title('The length of the loan expressed in months');
  • The Term parameter have 3 possible values (12, 36 , 60) Months
  • Most of loans are 36 Months loans

Univariate Exploration: LoanStatus Feature¶

Definition: The current status of the loan: Cancelled, Chargedoff, Completed, Current, Defaulted, FinalPaymentInProgress, PastDue. The PastDue status will be accompanied by a delinquency bucket.

In [19]:
sb.countplot(data = df_after200907, y = 'LoanStatus', color = default_color).set_title('The current status of the loan');
  • From this plot you can see that most of the loans are in current state.

Univariate Exploration: LenderYield Feature¶

Definition: The Lender yield on the loan. Lender yield is equal to the interest rate on the loan less the servicing fee.

In [24]:
sb.violinplot(data = df_after200907, y = 'LenderYield').set_title('Lender yield on the loan');
  • from the plot you can see that the LenderYield is distributed evenly, many loans have linder yield 0.182701

Univariate Exploration: EstimatedEffectiveYield Feature¶

Definition: Effective yield is equal to the borrower interest rate (i) minus the servicing fee rate, (ii) minus estimated uncollected interest on charge-offs, (iii) plus estimated collected late fees. Applicable for loans originated after July 2009.

In [25]:
sb.boxplot(data = df_after200907, y = 'EstimatedEffectiveYield').set_title('Estimated Effective Yield');
  • Mean: 0.168677
  • 25% quartile : 0.11567
  • 75% quartile : 0.2243

Univariate Exploration: EstimatedLoss Feature¶

Definition: Estimated loss is the estimated principal loss on charge-offs. Applicable for loans originated after July 2009.

In [26]:
sb.boxplot(data = df_after200907, y = 'EstimatedLoss').set_title('Estimated principal loss on charge-offs');
  • The estimated loss mean around 8%

Univariate Exploration: EstimatedReturn Feature¶

Definition: The estimated return assigned to the listing at the time it was created. Estimated return is the difference between the Estimated Effective Yield and the Estimated Loss Rate. Applicable for loans originated after July 2009.

In [27]:
sb.violinplot(data = df_after200907, y = 'EstimatedReturn').set_title('Estimated Return');
  • The average EstimatedReturn is 9.6%
